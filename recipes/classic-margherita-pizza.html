Classic Margherita Pizza

A timeless pizza recipe that celebrates the simplicity of traditional Italian cuisine. A thin crust is topped with a luscious layer of tomato sauce, crowned with fresh mozzarella slices, and adorned with aromatic basil leaves. Baked to crispy perfection, this Margherita pizza offers a delightful blend of flavors that showcases the essence of the Mediterranean kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500°F/260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and transfer it to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the sliced mozzarella over the sauce, and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven.
  6. Bake for about 8-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and top with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice and enjoy your classic Margherita pizza!
