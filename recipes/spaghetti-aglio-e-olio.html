Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A classic Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti cooked to perfection and tossed in a fragrant blend of thinly sliced garlic, extra-virgin olive oil, and a hint of red pepper flakes for a mild kick. Finished with fresh parsley and optional grated Parmesan cheese, this simple yet flavorful recipe is a delightful comfort food. Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, and saut√© until the garlic turns golden brown (be careful not to burn it).
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss well with the garlic-infused oil. If the pasta seems dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water to create a sauce.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle chopped parsley over the top and toss once more.
  5. Serve immediately, with optional grated Parmesan cheese on top.
